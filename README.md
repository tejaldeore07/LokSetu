LokSetu is an AI-powered civic intelligence and community issue resolution platform designed to help citizens, local communities, and municipal stakeholders report, verify, monitor, and resolve hyperlocal civic problems in a transparent way. The platform supports photo and video-based issue reporting, AI-assisted categorization, geolocation tagging, department routing, community validation, real-time status tracking, impact dashboards, and civic engagement features that encourage public participation and accountability.

What makes LokSetu more powerful is its broader civic monitoring workflow. Citizens can verify issues posted in their nearby ward, helping the community confirm whether a problem is genuine, urgent, or already being addressed. LokSetu also supports social-media-driven civic reporting, where public posts from platforms such as Instagram, X/Twitter, or Facebook can be turned into actionable civic reports. The system can detect relevant issue signals, extract the photo or evidence, and route the complaint to the appropriate department such as roads, sanitation, water supply, or electricity.

In addition, LokSetu includes a Scan News feature that monitors civic complaints and local concerns through NewsAPI, GNews, NewsData.io, and free RSS feeds for a selected area. Before anything becomes an official complaint, Gemini analyzes the article text and image content to understand the issue context, helping reduce noise and improve reliability. This creates a smarter pipeline where public news, social updates, and citizen reports all contribute to a verified civic issue workflow.

Built with Flask and Gemini API, LokSetu demonstrates how AI can improve community problem-solving by making reporting easier, prioritization smarter, verification stronger, and resolution more transparent. The project aims to bridge the gap between citizens and authorities by turning scattered complaints into structured, trackable civic action.
